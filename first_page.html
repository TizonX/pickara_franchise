Benefits of Master Franchise

He can sell sub franchises and will get the 30% of total sale value.

Head office will generate the Sub Franchise leads for him and can also fix the meeting but then the company will Take 90% as share.

He can run the whole city by himself only.

Commission sharing will be 3:1, where company will keep 1 and franchise will get 3. Eg- product cost 100, Commission from merchant 15%, Company share:- 3% of 15% and Franchise share:- 12% of 15%.

Franchises will get 2% and sub franchises will get 10%. 2% all time royalty. (ref point-4)

Master Franchise and Sub-Franchise can buy branding Material and DM services directly from the Company. There will be additional cost charged for these services.

Master franchise can charge money from the sub franchise for providing marketing resources. (If Required).

10% Share from merchant registration charges.
